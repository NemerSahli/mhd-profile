Welcome to Rising Horizon for Trading

ABOUT COMPANY

Based in Lebanon, Rising Horizon for Trading Off-shore SAL specializes in connecting global suppliers with developing markets in Southwest Africa. We offer a diverse range of products, including Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) and industrial raw materials, ensuring top quality and reliability in every transaction.

Vision

To be the leading trading partner between global suppliers and Southwest African markets, delivering unparalleled value through efficient and transparent operations.

Mission

To streamline international trade by sourcing premium products worldwide and delivering them to Southwest Africa with excellence in customer service and operational efficiency.
